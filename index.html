Cloud sync

Progress syncs to your private GitHub gist — across phone & desktop.
One-time, on each device — use the account that owns the gist (pakocica):
  1. Open github.com → new classic token (the gist scope is pre-checked)
  2. Pick an expiry, Generate token, copy it (ghp_…)
  3. Paste it above and hit Connect.
A classic token with the gist scope is the reliable choice — fine-grained tokens often 403 on gists. Stored only in this browser; used only for your one progress gist.

Recall level assumed for the faked units' cards — drives the previewed weather, ember rings, urgency pulse and treasure shine. Slide low to summon the storm.

Power-mean exponent for aggregating per-card recall into unit/course recall. Negative σ makes weak cards dominate — the forecast turns pessimistic about anything half-forgotten.
